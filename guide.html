How to Order

1

Find Your Product

Browse our categories by vehicle make and model, or use the search function to find specific products. You can also filter by product type such as Aero Parts, Interior, Exterior, LED Products, etc.

2

Select Options

Choose your preferred color, material, or size options if available. Check product specifications to ensure compatibility with your vehicle.

3

Add to Cart

Click "Add to Cart" button. You can continue shopping or proceed to checkout. Review your cart to confirm quantities and options.

4

Checkout

Enter your shipping address and select your preferred shipping method. Choose your payment method and complete the order.

Payment Methods

We accept the following payment methods:

Credit/Debit Cards

Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and other major cards are accepted. All transactions are secured with SSL encryption.

PayPal

Pay securely with your PayPal account. You don't need to share your financial information with us.

Bank Transfer

Available for select regions. Contact our customer service for bank transfer details.

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you will receive a confirmation email with your tracking number. You can track your order by:

  • Clicking the tracking link in your shipping confirmation email
  • Logging into your account and viewing Order History
  • Contacting our customer service with your order number

Returns & Refunds

Return Policy

  • Returns accepted within 14 days of delivery
  • Items must be unused and in original packaging
  • Custom or special orders may not be eligible for return
  • Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer (unless defective)

How to Request a Return

  1. Contact us at koreaauto21@gmail.com with your order number
  2. Describe the reason for return
  3. Wait for return authorization and shipping instructions
  4. Ship the item back to us with the authorization number

Refund Processing

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item. The refund will be credited to your original payment method.

Account Management

With a Korea Car Accessory account, you can:

  • Track your orders and view order history
  • Save multiple shipping addresses
  • Create a wishlist for future purchases
  • Receive personalized product recommendations
  • Get exclusive member discounts and early access to sales

To create an account, click Sign Up and fill in your information.
