Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary depending on your location. You can see the exact shipping cost at checkout before completing your order.

Shipping times vary by destination: North America (7-14 days standard, 3-5 days express), Europe (10-18 days standard, 4-7 days express), Asia Pacific (5-12 days standard, 2-4 days express). Please note that customs processing may add additional time.

Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number. You can use this number to track your package on the carrier's website. You can also track your order by logging into your account and viewing your Order History.

Depending on your country's regulations, you may be required to pay customs duties, taxes, or import fees. These charges are determined by your local customs authority and are the responsibility of the recipient. We recommend checking with your local customs office for more information.

We accept major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for select regions. All transactions are processed securely with SSL encryption.

Yes, we use industry-standard SSL encryption to protect your payment information. We do not store your complete credit card details on our servers. All payment processing is handled by trusted third-party payment providers.

We accept returns within 14 days of delivery for unused items in original packaging. Custom or special order items may not be eligible for return. To initiate a return, please contact us with your order number.

Contact us at koreaauto21@gmail.com with your order number and reason for return. We'll provide you with return authorization and shipping instructions. Return shipping costs are the customer's responsibility unless the item is defective.

Once we receive and inspect your returned item, refunds are processed within 5-7 business days. The refund will be credited to your original payment method. Please note that it may take additional time for the refund to appear on your statement depending on your bank.

We offer a mix of genuine OEM parts and high-quality aftermarket accessories. Each product description clearly indicates whether it's an OEM part or aftermarket accessory. All our products are sourced from trusted Korean manufacturers.

Each product listing includes compatibility information specifying which vehicle models and years the product fits. If you're unsure, please contact us with your vehicle details (make, model, year) and we'll help you find the right product.

Most products include basic installation instructions. For complex installations, we recommend professional installation. Some products may have installation guides or videos available on our website or upon request.
